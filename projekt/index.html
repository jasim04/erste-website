Program details

The JetBrains Startup Program allows startups to purchase up to 10 IDE licenses, 10 paid Team/Learning Tool subscriptions, and 10 AI Assistant Pro licenses at 50% off over a 5-year period.

We want to get you off to a great start - check to see if your company meets the requirements:

  • Your company is privately held
  • You can confirm that it was established less than 5 years ago.
  • Your company has a website or existing public references on the internet.

Partner program for startup incubators and accelerators

JetBrains works with incubators and accelerators who foster startups.

If you are an incubator-led startup, you can apply for the Startup Program under special conditions. Just make sure your incubator is already participating in the partner program.
